Rules & Format

The highest score you can card is +5 on a hole. Everything outside of the leather must be putted. Play everything as a lateral, including hazards marked as OB. You are permitted to bump the ball from a divot in the fairway, but play everything else down.

You must play all six rounds to be eligible to win the King Classic and have your name on the trophy. The winner will have the lowest net score over six rounds.

Side Games

You have the opportunity to join a skins game (based on net low score), which is $5 per round and then split among the participants based on the number of skins won.

Closest to the pin is $5 per round. The hole has been pre-selected and is identified above.

There is no obligation to be in either side game, but if you are joining, Alex is the bank, so settle up with him.

Teams

Each round an individual player will put in $10. At the beginning of each round, all 16 players will draw a number from a hat to see who their partner is. The top three two-man teams with the lowest net combined score will win the cash prize accordingly:

First place: $80

Second place: $50

Third place: $30
